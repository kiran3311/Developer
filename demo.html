TVS Apache RR 310

The TVS Apache RR 310 ( RR : Race Replica ) is a sport bike made by the TVS Motor Company in collaboration with BMW Motorrad that was launched in India on December 6, 2017 .[2] It uses a 312.2 cc (19.05 cu in) single-cylinder, four-stroke, SI, liquid-cooled, DOHC reverse inclined engine.

Although the engine of the Apache RR 310 is from BMW Motorrad's BMW G310 R platform, the motorcycle was entirely designed by TVS in India. It is the company's first fully-faired production motorcycle.
